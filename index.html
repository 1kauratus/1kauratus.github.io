Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions

Using the 3D Globe

How do I navigate the 3D globe?

Click and drag to rotate the globe, use mouse wheel to zoom in/out, and hover over property markers to see details.

What do the property markers represent?

Each glowing marker represents a property listing. The color and size may indicate price range or property type.

Finding Properties

How do I filter properties?

Use the filters on the Properties page to narrow down by location, price, bedrooms, bathrooms, and more.

Can I save favorite properties?

Yes, click the heart icon on any property to save it to your favorites list for later viewing.

Getting Support

How can I contact an agent?

Use the contact form or call our support line. Our agents are available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM.

Do you offer virtual tours?

Yes, many of our properties offer 360° virtual tours and video walkthroughs.
